Cancellation and Refund
The foody (buyer) is entitled to a full refund if he cancels a meal upto until 3 hours before the 'Food Ready By' time, i.e. the food delivery/pickup time. If the payment for the order was made online (through Netbanking, Credit/Debit card, Wallets or some other online payment method), the foody will receive a refund to his/her account within 5-7 business days, in concurrence with the refund request. Any order cancelled by the foody at a time which is less than 3 hours before the 'Food Ready by Time', the foody will not receive any refund as chefs (buddies) would have started preparations for getting the food ready. If the order is placed with the Cash on Delivery option and cancelled by the Foody at a time which is less than 3 hours before 'Food Ready by Time', the cost of such cancelled order will be added to the next order placed by the foody.
To cancel an order, please go to 'My Orders' and click on your order. You will see an option to call the chef. Please click on the Call button to call the chef and cancel the order. If you are unable to get through to the chef, you can go to 'Contact Us' under the hamburger and call Foodhousie to cancel the order.
