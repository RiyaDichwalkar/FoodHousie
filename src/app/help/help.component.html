  • RETURNS

    Refund Terms

    A refund or replacement request should be initiated within 7 days of date of delivery. A refund or replacement will be initiated for following cases

    • Quality issues
    • Quantity mismatch
    • Items not as per description

    For refunds, the amount will be credited back within 5 days of pickup confirmation For replacements, the new delivery will be made as per SLA policy Refund terms for sellers Post confirmation from us, you will have to arrange pickup of requested items within 1 day. For replacements, seller will have to arrange the same within 1 day of pickup Payment terms for buyers Online payments Can use any online payment method (card, netbanking, UPI, etc) Can use any payment wallets Cash on delivery Pay directly to your Chef

    A4. Yes. You will get your invoice from our supplier at the time of delivery.
